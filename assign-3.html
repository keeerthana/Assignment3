Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

By Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres

    

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in Louvre.It is the Third of the three portrits of the Riviere family the artist painted that year.
